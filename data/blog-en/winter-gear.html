Winter mountaineering in Iran demands specialized gear. Cold, wind and ice multiply risk — the right equipment is not optional.

Clothing

  • Merino or synthetic base layers — no cotton
  • Insulated mid layer (fleece or down)
  • Waterproof breathable shell
  • Down or synthetic belay jacket for stops
  • Balaclava, goggles, glacier glasses

Footwear & traction

  • Insulated mountaineering boots (B2/B3 for technical routes)
  • Crampons matched to boots
  • Gaiters against snow ingress

Tools & safety

  • Ice axe on slopes above 30°
  • Helmet under seracs or on rock-ice mix
  • Avalanche transceiver, probe and shovel in avalanche terrain
  • Headlamp with cold-rated batteries
⚠️ Winter rule
If you have not used crampons and ice axe before, take a course or climb with an experienced leader. Gear without skills does not keep you safe.
