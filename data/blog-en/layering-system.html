In the mountains you can go from 25°C at the trailhead to near freezing on the summit in one day. The three-layer system is the best way to manage these swings.

Three layers

1. Base layer

Avoid cotton! Polypropylene or merino wicks moisture away from skin.

2. Mid layer

Fleece or light puffy for insulation — add or remove as temperature changes.

3. Shell layer

Windproof and waterproof jacket. On windy ridges, wind chill is more dangerous than the thermometer reading.

What to carry

  • One extra warm layer even in summer
  • Thin gloves + warm gloves
  • Hat and neck gaiter
