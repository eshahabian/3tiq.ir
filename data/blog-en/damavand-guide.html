Mount Damavand is Iran and the Middle East's highest peak, the world's seventh highest volcano, and a cultural symbol of Iran. Thousands of climbers attempt it every year — this guide helps you prepare for your best ascent.

Ascent routes

Southern route (Rine) — most popular

Starts from Rine village and Gosfandsara shelter (3,050 m). Lowest risk, best infrastructure — recommended for most climbers.

  • Day 1: Rine → Gosfandsara (3,050 m) — 2 hours
  • Day 2: Gosfandsara → Bargah-e Sevom (4,220 m) — 4 hours
  • Day 3: Bargah → summit (5,610 m) → descent — 8 hours
💡 Important tip
One night at Gosfandsara before moving to high camp greatly helps acclimatization and reduces AMS risk.

Northern route (Polur)

Starts from the north (Polur), more technical and demanding. The north ridge holds permanent glaciers.

Shelters

Three main shelters on the southern route:

  • Gosfandsara (3,050 m): 100 beds, electricity, water, kitchen
  • Bargah-e Sevom (4,220 m): 80 beds, solar power, water
  • High camp above Bargah (4,800 m): tents only, no facilities

Required gear

  • Proper mountaineering boots (3-season minimum)
  • Crampons (mandatory on north route and winter)
  • Two trekking poles
  • Three-layer warm clothing
  • Windproof waterproof gloves
  • Wool hat and face mask
  • UV-protective sunglasses
  • First-aid kit + Diamox (doctor's prescription only)
  • Food and water for 3 days
⚠️ Safety warning
Never climb Damavand alone. Minimum group of 3 and telling family your plan is essential. Weather above 4,500 m can change violently within hours.

Best season

Best window is July through late September when winter snow has melted, weather is stable, shelters are open, and avalanche risk is lowest.

From October onward temperatures drop sharply; December–May is for experienced winter climbers only.

Final tips

  • Spend 2–3 days at 2,000–3,000 m before the climb to acclimatize
  • Start summit day at 2–3 AM to reach the top before afternoon storms
  • Headache, nausea or unusual fatigue — turn back immediately
  • Pack out all your trash
